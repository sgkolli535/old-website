Student

Awaken to the sun's rays glaring through the open window

Stagger through the yellow lights and clothing heaps of the bathroom

Stumble down the carpeted staircase onto the busy kitchen table

Ignore the grumbles of your stomach and the beckoning of the refrigerator

Reach for the topmost in a dangerously tilting stack of books

Bring yourself to open the book large enough to house all of your regrets

Begin reading the words on the page: "Questions 1-10 are based on..."

Try to focus on the passage that slowly turns into a whirlwind of words

Sit there for hours toiling through the countless questions and choices

Feel ensnared between your diverging requirements and desires

Rise from the congested table after intense inner deliberation

Hoist up the dense workbook and along with it your final restraints

Hurl the bound papers into the air and into the desolate dwelling around you

Marvel at the sheets soaring with your spirits at that moment

Forget their warnings: “Studying is the only thing you can do”

Revel in the sovereignty of the undone shackles of your aspirations

Realize, at last, the honest nature of your dreams, rather their non-existence

Start gathering the remnants of the workbook and your obsolete hopes

Be seated once again at the table present for your short-lived revelation

Reopen the pages with words that now seem to taunt your naivety

Resume your emotionless, soulless, meaningless journey lost more than ever
