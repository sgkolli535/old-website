About Me

Hello! My name is Sumedha Kolli, or Sumi for short. I made this website to showcase my interests, experiences, and some of my creative writing pieces in a format I like to call "loose-leaf", representing the ideas and thoughts that drift through my mind.

I am a freshman at USC studying Computer Science/Business and minoring in Applied Analytics, a combination where I can explore the intersection of business concepts and computing logic. Originally from Atlanta, I came to the west coast to explore both my professional and personal interests. One of my biggest passions is creative writing and poetry, where I find the freedom to explore and express my thoughts and ideas. Other than that, I am a huge Sneakerhead and an advocate of Sustainable Living.

Thank you for visiting my website, and feel free to explore the rest of the site to learn more about me!
