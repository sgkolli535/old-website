As spring arose to divulge her playful head,

The last of the lingering gloom had fled.

Day by Day the youthful spirits soared,

And the initial content was now restored.

With the weight of duty gently assuaged,

Their young minds no longer felt caged.

There was relief...

While prior dilemmas lost their cutting sting,

A lucid perspective came into full-being.

At long last fond memories took flight,

Just as the daylight swept through the night.

Such bitter beliefs and sour sentiments,

Were traded for yearnings of the past in relevance.

There was longing...

The time had arrived for celebration and rejoice,

Beside a world full of promise, fortune, and choice.

What previously resembled a shapeless haze,

Was steadily illuminated by the shining sun's rays.

A curious energy scattered throughout the crowd;

Only the future held its identity to be found.

There was hope...
